# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises. It covers eight personas across core delivery, design, data, release, customer success, and security functions.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
Ensures user needs and usability are prioritized throughout the product development lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes
- Collaborate with Product Managers and Developers to align design with requirements
- Participate in design reviews and incorporate feedback
- Maintain design systems and style guides
- Validate designs through user testing and metrics

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible, and user-centered experiences
- Reduce user friction and improve satisfaction metrics
- Ensure design consistency across features

### Typical Communication
- Design review sessions with developers and product managers
- User research findings and testing reports
- Design documentation and prototypes in collaboration tools
- Sprint planning and backlog refinement

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Defines key metrics, builds data dashboards, and provides data-driven insights to inform product and project decisions.

### Responsibilities
- Define success metrics and KPIs for features and projects
- Build and maintain data dashboards and reporting tools
- Validate data integrity for releases and feature launches
- Analyze usage patterns, trends, and customer behavior
- Support Product and Project Managers with data to inform prioritization
- Identify opportunities for optimization through data analysis

### Goals
- Enable data-driven decision making across teams
- Ensure measurement and tracking of success metrics
- Provide actionable insights from data analysis

### Typical Communication
- Weekly metric reviews with product and project teams
- Data analysis reports and dashboard updates
- Collaboration on defining success criteria and KPIs
- Ad-hoc analysis requests and findings

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
Owns release planning and coordinates deployment activities to ensure smooth, reliable production rollouts.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain release schedules and deployment plans
- Coordinate QA testing and sign-offs before releases
- Manage communication between Dev, QA, Product, and stakeholders
- Oversee deployment execution and post-release verification
- Maintain release documentation and runbooks
- Coordinate rollback procedures when needed
- Track release metrics and identify process improvements

### Goals
- Deliver zero-downtime deployments with minimal incidents
- Maintain clear visibility into release status and timelines
- Continuously improve release processes and automation

### Typical Communication
- Release planning meetings with engineering and product
- Pre-release go/no-go decisions with stakeholders
- Release announcements and status updates
- Post-release retrospectives and incident reviews

---

## Customer Success Lead

### Role Summary
Bridges the delivery team with customers and end-users, ensuring successful product adoption and gathering feedback for continuous improvement.

### Responsibilities
- Gather customer feedback, use cases, and pain points
- Collaborate with Product and Project teams to communicate customer needs
- Assist with customer onboarding, training, and documentation
- Monitor customer health metrics and identify at-risk accounts
- Advocate for customer needs in product planning and prioritization
- Coordinate beta programs and early access initiatives
- Document customer success stories and best practices

### Goals
- Maximize customer satisfaction, retention, and lifetime value
- Reduce time to value for new customers
- Ensure customer voice influences product decisions

### Typical Communication
- Weekly updates on customer feedback and priorities
- Customer escalation coordination with engineering and product
- Onboarding materials and customer-facing documentation
- Quarterly business reviews with key accounts

---

## Security Lead

### Role Summary
Reviews features for security requirements, coordinates threat assessments, and ensures compliance throughout the development lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Review design and implementation for security vulnerabilities
- Define security requirements and acceptance criteria
- Coordinate security assessments and penetration testing
- Ensure compliance with security standards and regulations
- Lead incident response for security events
- Maintain security documentation and runbooks
- Provide security training and guidance to development teams
- Monitor security scanning results and coordinate remediation

### Goals
- Prevent security vulnerabilities from reaching production
- Maintain compliance with security standards and regulations
- Build security awareness and best practices into team culture

### Typical Communication
- Security review checkpoints during planning and development
- Threat model reviews and security assessment findings
- Incident response coordination during security events
- Security training sessions and guideline updates

---

## How these personas are used in the exercise
- Use these persona definitions to frame scenarios and sample interactions in the Skills Exercise.
- Each persona can be used as a persona prompt for Copilot Spaces to shape role-specific guidance.
- The eight personas span core delivery (Developers, Product Managers, Project Managers), design and data (UX Designer, Data Analyst), and specialized functions (Release Manager, Customer Success Lead, Security Lead).
